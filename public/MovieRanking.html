~ The Best Animated Movies According To Me ~

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

  • Your Name

    Watched this movies in my early teens. Beautiful story and animation with an amazing sound track.

  • Weathering With You

    Again, love the animation story and soundtrack. will absolutely reccomend.

  • Summer Ghost

    A simple movie about three friends who bonded after summoning a infamous ghost who was often sighted during summer. Highlights the beauty of life.
